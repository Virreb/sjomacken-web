Welcome!

The station is located in the guest harbour of Hunnebostrand. Here you can refuel petrol and diesel throughout the whole year, the diesel is marine diesel and thus RME free. During high season (approximately mid-June to early-August), the station is staffed from 09:00 - 18:00 with the availability to purchase accessories, ice cream and coffee, or rent a boat, kayak or jet-ski. For the rest of the year and evenings only refueling is possible via a card machine.

Boat rental

We have five boats with around the same engine size as we rent. Rental includes anchors, life jackets, oars, rope and a map.

The price is 500SEK for half a day (09-13 or 14-18) or 800SEK for one whole day (09-18). The cost of gasoline is added, the tank is full when you get the boat and is refilled when you return.

It is possible to rent during other periods, then the price is set by agreement. Security in form of a ID-card and a lease contract signed on-site are required before rental.

Kayak rental

We have three types of kayaks; four single kayaks of two different lengths and two double kayaks. In one of the double kayaks there is also a smaller third seat for a child or pet. Rental includes a map, life jackets, paddles, splash covers and a compass. We will of course help you set up the kayaks, show how they work and launch them.

The price for a one-man kayak (single) is 300SEK for half a day (09-13 or 14-18), 400SEK for one day (09-18) or 500SEK for a day and night.

The price for a two-man kayak (double) is 450SEK for half a day (09-13 or 14-18), 550SEK for one day (09-18) or 700SEK for a day and night.

It is possible to rent during other periods, then the price is set by agreement. Security in form of a ID-card and a lease contract signed on-site are required before rental.

Jet-ski rental

In cooperation with Börjessons ATV we rent out two Sea-Doo Sparks, this model can accommodate two people (max 160kg).

The price is 600SEK for half an hour or 1000SEK for an hour. The cost of gasoline is added, they use about 10 liters per hour depending on how fast you drive. Life jackets are included.

It is possible to rent during other periods, then the price is set by agreement. Security in form of a ID-card and a lease contract signed on-site are required before rental. It's necessary to be above 18 years of age or in company of a guardian to rent.

Gas and accessories

We have a small selection of accessories and oils for Volvo Penta. If something is missing or not working, we have a partnership with a Volvo Penta certified marina.

The gas comes from AGA and has the Swedish gas connection (as well as some European G2 and G3). If your bottle-type is not available it can be ordered to the next delivery. We only exchange bottles, it is therefore not possible to only submit one bottle. Nevertheless, it's possible to switch between different bottle types.

We operate as a pick-up point for SeaSea Megastore in Smögen. They have one of Bohuslän's largest selection of boat accessories which can be ordered for next day.
